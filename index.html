Will-O'-the-Wisp (W.O.W.) Fire Protection is a new, full-service outfit founded by two seasoned technicians serving all of Oregon and Washington for all of your fire equipment needs. Fully licensed, bonded, and insured; we provide routine inspection, testing, and maintenance services, and 24/7 emergency response service for portable extinguishers, fixed-pipe suppression systems, backflow prevention apparatus, fire sprinklers, and other water-based fire protection equipment. Alongside our above-listed services, we specialize in the installation of kitchen and dry chemical industrial suppression systems.
